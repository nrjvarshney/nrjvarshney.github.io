Research Statement

  • I work in Natural Language Processing / Understanding, primarily towards addressing different Efficiency and Reliability aspects.

    Specifically, I focus on problems such as Mitigating the Hallucinations of LLMs, Efficient LLM Decoding, Instruction Tuning, Question Answering, Inference Efficiency, Text Generation, Selective Prediction, Training Sample Efficiency, Large Language Models, and Retrieval Augmented Inference.
